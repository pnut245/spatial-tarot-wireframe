Tarot Wheel
Dart draw prototype (graphic/print)

Question / intention

Two rings: outer = Major Arcana, inner = Minor Arcana. Drag a ring to turn it, then throw a dart to select both.
DART
Spin strength 6
Pointer is fixed; rings rotate underneath. Drag near the edge for Majors, nearer the center for Minors.

Selected

Throw a dart to select a card.

How it works

  • Major Arcana = outer ring (22 slices).
  • Minor Arcana = inner ring (56 slices).
  • Throwing a dart spins both rings and selects a pair.
